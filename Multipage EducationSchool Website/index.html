Frequently Asked Questions

What is the medium of instruction?

The medium of instruction at Masai is English. All lectures are delivered in English. As part of the curriculum, we also help you develop your written and verbal communication skills that help you become more employable.

Where is EGATOR located?

The Masai School head office is located in Bengaluru, India. Both our Web Development and Data Analytics courses are held live online in an interactive environment.

Does EGATOR help me get a job?

The Web Development & Data Analytics space is amongst the most in-demand and ever-growing sectors in India. Our in-house placements team is dedicated to launching your career. It works with over 1400 placement partners like MPL, Dream11, Samsung, Instamojo, Paytm, Nobroker, ShareChat, Revvsales etc. Our executive will help you prepare your resume, portfolio, build your digital profile, practice interviews and even help you when it comes to salary negotiation. However, at the end of the day, you are the one who would apply, give the interview and get the job.

Is attendance mandatory?

Yes, a minimum of 90% attendance is required to complete our courses. You also need to follow the guidelines mentioned in EGATOR Code of Conduct Policy. Please go through this link to understand Code of Conduct policy* *relaxations are provided to part time programs (working professionals and final year students)

Who is eligible to apply for a EGATOR course?

To be eligible for a course you must-have : General- Courses are for India Residents only. You must have a valid PAN card and Aadhaar Card. You are required to have a laptop and a good internet connection. Basic knowledge of the English language is a must for our Web Development Courses You need to be available to take a job immediately after course completion. Age- Software Development Courses: 18-28 years. Data Analytics: 18-28 years. Academic Background- If you have cleared the 12th standard under CBSE/ICSE/State Recognised board of education. You need no prior coding or design skills to join our course. College dropouts may also apply. Part-Time Courses are available only for students graduating in 2022. Entrance Test- For Software Development: You have 1 attempt to clear the test. On not clearing the MSAT, you can apply to the next batch.

Does college CGPA play any role in the program?

No, your college CGPA does not play any role in your entry to our courses.

What computer specs do I need?

For our Web Development Courses: Minimum 1.6 GHz Processor 4 GB RAM**(For Full stack Android development - 8 GB RAM)** 120 GB Hard Drive Web Cam Microphone ‍ Recommended 2 GHz Processor 8 GB RAM 256 GB Hard Drive (preferably solid state) ‍ Please Note: Recommended operating systems (in descending order): Ubuntu, macOS, Windows 10 For our UI/UX Design Courses: Minimum Intel i3 1.6 GHz Processor 4 GB RAM 120 GB Hard Drive Web Cam Microphone Recommended Intel i5 and above 2 GHz Processor 8 GB RAM 256 GB Hard Drive (preferably solid state) Note: You must have a Windows 10 or above operating system OR a Mac system with iOS 10 and above for the design softwares to run.

What do you look for in candidates when they apply ?

EGATOR is a fast-paced, immersive, online education experience. It's important to ask yourself, "Is this right for me?" Below are a few of the qualities it will take to be successful in our courses: Determination Proactiveness Career-focussed

What do I do if I don't have a Pan Card/Adhaar Card?

At the time of admission, having an Aadhaar is mandatory. PAN Card is not mandatory at the time of admission
